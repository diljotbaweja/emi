ELECTROMAGNETIC INDUCTION

The phenomenon of generation of electricity by changing magnetic flux of coil is known as EMI.
The voltage induced is called INDUCED EMF . Current flowing is called INDUCED CURRENT.
MAGNETIC FLUX:
Total no. of magnetic lines crossing a surface.
It is given by PHI =nB cos theta
where B=magnetic field
A=Area of cross section
theta is angle between B--> and A-->.
